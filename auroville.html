Auroville Website Design

This project is a redesign of Auroville website. Auroville is a city in India which is famously known for its harmony and peace which is not bounded by any caste, religion or culture.

My vision here was to create a website which will reflect on this mission and promote the city for Tourism.

Research Analysis

It was essential to analyze the positive and negative from the current design. Apparently I realized the flaws and worked thoroughly to eliminate ensuring the purpose of the website.

  • Excess Textual Information
  • Lack of Visual harmony
  • Not Promoting Tourism
  • Imbalanced White Space
  • Improper Font-size
  • Repetition
  • Unrelated Content
  • Good choice of Color Palette
  • Informative Footer

Incorporation of Text

The most confusing task was to incorporate the enormous amount of data that the Auroville team wanted to include in website without compromising on the visuals.
Hence, I decided to create overlay with appropriate background in hover state, which gives the information to the user only when required.
